About Us

We are a digital collective of visionaries, strategists, and creators. We don't just build websites; we craft digital legacies suitable for the modern age.

Our Story

From a Coffee Shop
to a Global Agency

Founded in 2020 by two designers who were tired of "good enough," Lumina began as a humble experiment in remote collaboration. We wanted to see what would happen if we prioritized user empathy above all else.

Fast forward to today, we've partnered with over 200 brands, ranging from scrappy fintech startups to Fortune 500 giants. Our mission remains unchanged: to humanize technology through data-driven, emotional design.
